About Glam-by Suzii

Discover the story behind our passion for beauty

Suzii's Journey

From a young age, Suzii was fascinated by the transformative power of makeup. What began as experimenting with her mother's makeup kit evolved into a lifelong passion for helping others discover their unique beauty.

After graduating from the prestigious Makeup Designory in Nairobi, Suzii worked with top beauty brands and celebrities, honing her skills in various makeup styles from natural beauty to high fashion editorial.

In 2020, Suzii founded Glam-by Suzii with a mission to make professional makeup artistry accessible to everyone, while celebrating individuality and self-expression.
